Chipotle Burrito

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • White Rice
  • Steak
  • Tomato
  • Red Onion
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Cilantro
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Lay down your tortilla
  2. Cover one half of the tortilla with rice
  3. Place the steak on top of the layer of rice
  4. Place the tomatoes, onions, and cilantro in a bowl
  5. Pour the lime juice and salt in the bowl and mix it all together
  6. Take that mixture and put it on top of the steak
  7. Layer the top with mozzarella
  8. Wrap your burrito
  9. Warm up if needed to allow the mozzarella to melt
