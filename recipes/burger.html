Smash Burger

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook your patties on the grill
  2. Once your patties are close to done place buns on grill to toast
  3. Place cheese on top of the patties to melt
  4. Take the buns and place them on a plate
  5. Lay the patties and on the bottom bun
  6. Layer the lettuce, pickles, tomatoes, and your onions on top of the patty
  7. Drizzle your sauce of choice on top and place top bun
