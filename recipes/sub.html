Subway Sub

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Salami
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Black Olives
  • Ranch

Steps

  1. Slice your bread in half and lay them side by side
  2. Begin laying down the salami, pepperoni, and the mozzarella
  3. Place sub in a toaster until it's slightly toasted and the cheese has melted
  4. Lay down your lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, and black olives
  5. Drizzle your ranch on top
  6. Bring the two slices together
